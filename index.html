Our Process

Our design process is simple.

  • Information Gathering

    We conduct a meeting to understand your needs and requirements; the purpose, main goals and target audience of your website.

  • Planning and Design

    Creation of sitemaps and website layout to give you the opportunity to judge how the entire website will look like and any changes made according to your preference.

  • Content Writing and Assembly

    Putting into writing the message you would like to communicate to your audience of your website. it is a process that is done continuously through the web development cycle.

  • Development

    We do all the exciting technical coding stuff blah blah... and your website gets built according to the agreed upon preferences.

  • Testing, Review and Launch

    We test your website to see if everything is precision perfect, deploy and launch the website and run a final test then hand. Your website is now free for the whole world to see.
